I am a grad student at the University of California at Riverside (advised by Prof. Amit Roy Chowdhury). I previously worked as a Research Assistant (advised by Prof. Vineeth N Balasubramanian's) at the Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad (IITH). My fields of research broadly include Transfer Learning, Causality, ExplainableAI, and Learning under Limited Supervision.
